About

Apple of Discord, is an ancient Greek myth that follows the tale of the golden apple dropped by the Greek goddess of Discord and strife Eris, at the wedding of Peleus (son of Aecus and king of the island Aegina) and Thetis (a sea nymph and a goddess of the sea) on Mount Pelion. Eris offended that she was the only god uninvited to the wedding dropped the apple evenly distanced among three well known and powerful goddesses Athena, Aphrodite, and Hera and the apple inscribed with the words “To the fairest” eventually led to a vanity-fueled argument among the three goddesses. Zeus God of Thunder (who would usually handle petty disputes) did not want to risk offending any of the goddesses so left the responsibility up to the Trojan prince, Paris to decide who the apple was intended for and therefore the most beautiful. Each of the three goddesses offered Paris a unique gift if he chose one of them. Athena offered unmatched wisdom and skill in war. Hera offered power and domination over all of Asia and lastly Aphrodite offered the most beautiful woman in the world. None other than Helen of Sparta (who was already married to King Menelaus of Sparta). Tempted by Aphrodite's offer, he chose to pursue Helen of Sparta by letting Aphrodite place a love enchantment on her and stealing her from her kingdom, eventually starting the Trojan war.

Species and Gender

Eris: Female goddess

The goddess of strife and discord, Eris throws the golden apple at the wedding of Peleus and Thetis.

Hera: Female goddess

The goddess of marriage, Hera is one of the three goddesses who claim the apple.

Athena: Female goddess

The goddess of wisdom and warfare, Athena is another of the three goddesses who want the apple.

Aphrodite: Female goddess

The goddess of love and beauty, Aphrodite is the third goddess who desires the apple and ultimately receives it.

Paris: male prince

A Trojan prince, Paris is asked to choose which of the three goddesses is the fairest and is given the apple.

Helen of Sparta: Female mortal

Helen is a human woman, the wife of Menelaus, King of Sparta. She is the central figure in the Trojan War, whose abduction by Paris, a Trojan prince, is the catalyst for the conflict.

Weapons

The Apple of Discord

Inscribed with the words “To the fairest” was a trick to stir up scandal upon the gods because of Eris’s spite towards the fact that she was the only uninvited god to the wedding. This small act eventually led to the trojan war. It is now also a saying for when something small turns catastrophic “Sowing the seeds of discord”
